About Me

Nice to meet you, I'm...

Nick (Yili) Wang, a front end developer based in Toronto. I am a recent graduate of Juno (formerly HackerYou) College of Technology.

Earning a Bachelor of Industrial Design from OCAD university gives me the perspective on user centered design, and the ability to communicate effectively with other designers.

Previously working in a team environment made me love the feeling of collaboration to accomplish a common goal.

Beside coding, I love cats, video games, and have an ich to always come up with cool ideas to amuse the people around me.

These are my skills, more to come!
